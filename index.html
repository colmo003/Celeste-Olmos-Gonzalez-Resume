I am a second-year Psychology student at the University of California-Riverside. I want to continue my education past UCR to pursue a career in clinical psychology. Psychology research is also very interesting to me because I feel that there are still many unanswered questions about psychology and other things related to it. There are many fields related to psychology that interest me but these two capture my attention the most. I am motivated by my background and culture because mental health is something that has been long neglected in Mexican culture. As a Latina, I feel that it is important to shed light on the lack of mental health awareness as well as advocate for a change in the stigmatization of seeking psychological help. I hope to achieve some of these goals in my career. I have already started being more open with my own family to help them understand why I am passionate about creating a change in the perception of mental health in society.
